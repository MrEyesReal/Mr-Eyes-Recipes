Falafel Sandwich recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 6 pita bread loaves
  • 10 cooked falafel balls, from homemade or store-bought falafel mix
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 medium cucumber, unpeeled and diced
  • 1 medium white or red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • Tahini sauce, homemade or store-bought, to taste.
    •  OPTIONAL
  • 3 sandwich pickles, sliced.
